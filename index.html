About Me

I’m Jennifer Blakney (she/her), a front-end developer based in Hamilton, Ontario. Previously I have enjoyed working as a copywriter for the cannabis industry, and QA app testing for SiriusXM. I got my certifications in web development at Juno College (Cohort 45) in 2022.

I’m confident in my HTML, CSS and JavaScript skills, as well as other front end technologies. I’m especially passionate about accessible design and software, and learning more about those practices. My combined experiences have taught me to communicate effectively, efficiently solve problems, and work well in a team.

When not coding, I’m usually cooking, baking, walking my two dogs around Hamilton, or watching television with my partner. If you want to get to know me on the internet, visit my Medium, or connect with me on Twitter and LinkedIn.
